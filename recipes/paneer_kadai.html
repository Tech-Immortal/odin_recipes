Paneeer Kadai

Ingredients

  • 30gms paneer diced in cubes
  • tbsp vegetable oil
  • tsp cumin seeds
  • medium onion thinly sliced
  • garlic finely chopped
  • tsp kashmiri chilli powder (or mild paprika)
  • tsp coriander powder
  • inch turmeric powder
  • medium green pepper thinly sliced
  • small tomato sliced
  • alt to taste
  • " ginger slivers for garnish
  • tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Method

  1. Heat oil in a large heavy bottom sauce pan over a medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and sizzle for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the onions and fry for 8-10 minutes.
  4. As they begin to soften add the garlic and fry for a few seconds.
  5. Add all the spice powders and stir for a further minute.
  6. If the spices stick to the base of the pan add a splash of water.
  7. Now add the peppers and mix with all the spices for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Add the tomatoes at this stage and cook until slightly softened.
  9. Now add the paneer cubes and stir well to coat with all the spices.
  10. Cook for a couple of minutes, season to taste.
  11. Add the ginger, lemon juice & garnish with fresh coriander.
  12. Serve warm with naan or chapatti.
